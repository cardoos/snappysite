Slow websites are Aggravating!

A slow website costs you money and Loses you customers.

Think back to the last time a slow website aggravated you...

You were probably on your phone searching for something and you thought you finally found the answer. The brief description on Google sounds great but you need to know more. You click the link and wait for the page to load but it takes too long and you hit the back button. The website loaded way too slow, you got impatient and you backed out of the page before you got what you needed.

The Problem...

The above issue is called a bad user-experience and unless you have a seriously niche business then you need a fast website, it needs to be snappy and responsive on phones and computers. Unfortunately until just recently it wasnt really a big deal, if your website was a little bloated but still had useful and accurate information then Google didnt really care. That is simply not the case now, in July 2018 Google released this article that spells it out in plain english.

Speed is now a landing page factor for Google Search and Ads
When real users have a slow experience on mobile, they're much less likely to find what they are looking for or purchase from you in the future. For many sites this equates to a huge missed opportunity, especially when more than half of visits are abandoned if a mobile page takes over 3 seconds to load...

The Solution

The truth is you probably have a really slick and flashy looking website, it might even be brand new. Unfortunately new does not equal fast and with more and more web searches coming from mobile device, your website needs to be fast. This is where I come in. I will give your existing website the boost it needs to give your customers the info they need and the great user-experience they deserve. I can often do this without making any changes to your existing websites design, you get to keep the layout that you already love and your customers recognize. The only thing that will change is how fast your website loads.
